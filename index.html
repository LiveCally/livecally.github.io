About Live Calligraphy

Ever dream of writing Chinese characters like a calligrapher? Now, you can practice Chinese brush calligraphy with your finger in iPad and iPhone.

Live Calligraphy uses the original strokes extracted from the master piece of ancient calligrapher in your practice, so that you can feel yourself like a real master of Chinese calligraphy, and fully enjoy the artistic charm of the art of calligraphy.

LanTing calligraphy scroll is generally considered as the best running script in ancient China, and Live Calligraphy provides full characters of LanTing in current release, wish you like them.
